Purpose of a Non-Profit Organisation?

The purpose of nonprofit organizations is generally to improve quality of life for others at a community, local, state, national, or even global level


The Feed A Child Foundation is the largest disaster response non-profit organisation of African origin on the African continent. The essence of our presence is to bring hope, food parcels, clothes and restore dignity to the most vulnerable


Feed a Child has many projects and is currently delivering meals to children throughout South Africa.

It is estimated that almost 10% of the South African population are children of no particular culture or ethical group, living below the poverty line and in circumstances beyond their control.
